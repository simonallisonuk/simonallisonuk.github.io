I suffered burn out, and now some I know also suffering. This is what helped me, first I list the issues I had:  

1. Exhaustion (be kind to yourself) 
2. Thoughts rushing through your mind/too many to control  
3. Distractions 
4. Things blocking
5. Music in your head you can't control  

So how did I fix all this?  

1. Relax and set a routine
2. You can't control every thought, you need to write them down
3. Try to be aware of the distractions, and cut them down, mostly for me is finding a quiet space.  
4. What is blocking your way forward? write this down 
5. Listen to these songs, and they will help you relax. 

There is two tools which have been helping me and you might use them all the time 

At first it was just pen and paper but I now use @MicrosoftToDo and @Outlook Calendar 

Using these two helps get those things done, and scheduled when YOU need to.
