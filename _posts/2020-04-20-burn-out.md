Strangest thing, I suffered burn out, and now some I know also suffering. For the biggest was #1 and took along time to get over.  

#1 Exhaustion (be kind to yourself) 
#2 Thoughts rushing through your mind/too many to control  
#3 Distractions 
#4 Things blocking
#5 Music in your head you can't control  

So how do we fix all this?  

#1 Relax 
#2 You can't control everything, write this down 
#3 Try to be aware of them, and cut them down 
#4 What is blocking your way forward? write this down 
#5 Listen to these songs

There is two tools which have been helping me and you might use them all the time 

At first it was just pen and paper 
But I now use @MicrosoftToDo and @Outlook Calendar 

Using these two helps get those things done, and scheduled when YOU need to.
